About

About

Iskra Car Wash is your reliable partner in car care! We are proud to present ourselves as professionals in the field of car detailing and car washing. With our extensive experience and passion for cars, we guarantee that your vehicle will look like new after a visit to our car wash.

Our skilled staff and state-of-the-art equipment allow us to provide the highest level of cleanliness and quality. We value your time, so we endeavor to provide a fast and efficient wash despite the size of your vehicle. Trust us to give your car the diamond shine and cleanliness it deserves.
